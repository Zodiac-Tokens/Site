Scarce Supply

There is only 21 million of each token - same as Bitcoin - and there will never be more of them. So each token has the potential to get to the price of at least 1 BTC!

The whole supply was minted on day one. So no mining by expensive machines that belong to super wealthy and no carbon emission!

Leveraging the smart contracts capability of Ethereum blockchain, each Zodiac Token is an ERC-20 compliant token which means you have the whole Ethereum ecosystem at your disposal!

The source code is verified on Etherscan and available for security checks and audits. You can safely HODL your tokens on any Ethereum wallet and trade them on popular exchanges!
